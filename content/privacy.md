**Last updated:** February 27, 2026

PulseInsight ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website pulseinsight.io. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site.

## Information We Collect

### Analytics Data
We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our site. This service collects:
- Pages visited and time spent on pages
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level)
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Referral source (how you found us)

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data. The information is aggregated and does not personally identify you. For more information on how Google handles this data, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Cookies
Our site uses cookies for:
- **Analytics cookies** (Google Analytics) to measure site performance
- **Preference cookies** to remember your theme setting (light/dark mode)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

## How We Use Information

We use analytics data solely to:
- Understand which content is most useful to our audience
- Improve site performance and user experience
- Make informed decisions about future content

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Affiliate Links & Third-Party Services

Some articles on PulseInsight contain affiliate links to third-party products and services. When you click these links:
- You may be redirected to a third-party website
- That website's own privacy policy applies
- We may receive a commission if you make a purchase, at no additional cost to you

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. We do not maintain any separate database of visitor information.

## Children's Privacy

PulseInsight is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
